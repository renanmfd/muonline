Level Prediction

This tool calculates the level you are going to be in the future based on the current xp you are getting. The number don't take into account experience drop each level because the impact on the final value would be minimal. This is just an estimate for level and shop items planning.

Leave the Mu Helper active on your spot of choice for some time (at least 15 minutes) to get an accuarate prediction.
