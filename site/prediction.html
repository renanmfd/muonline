Level Prediction

This tool calculates the level you are going to be in the future based on the current xp you are getting. The number don't take into account experience drop each level. This is just an estimate for level and shop items planning.

Because of the lack of online information about experience for level up in levels above 800, this tool only calculates for levels bellow 800. This will be expanded to GMO max level (S15.2) 1200 in the short future.

Leave the Mu Helper active on your spot of choice for some time (at least 15 minutes) to get an accuarate prediction.
